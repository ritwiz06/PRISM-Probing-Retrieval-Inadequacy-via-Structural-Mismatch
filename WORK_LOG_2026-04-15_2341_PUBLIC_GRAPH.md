## Public Graph Summary

Artifacts:

- `data/processed/public_graph_triples.jsonl`
- `data/processed/public_graph.ttl`
- `data/processed/public_graph_metadata.json`

Current graph size:

- `49` extracted triples
- `20` public source documents represented
- average extraction confidence `0.841`
- pattern counts: `source_profile=48`, `is_a=1`

Extraction is intentionally lightweight and local. It uses source-aware profiles for known public demo pages plus conservative text patterns for simple public-text facts. The demo KG remains untouched and remains the production default.

## Known Limitations

- The public graph is small and source-selected.
- Extraction is rule/profile-based, not a general information extraction system.
- Public-graph-only currently matches demo KG on the benchmark because the suite is intentionally grounded in the extracted public graph.
- Mixed mode should be interpreted as curated support plus public structure, not proof that public extraction alone is complete.
- Entity and relation normalization are controlled and can miss paraphrases outside the known aliases/predicates.
- Answer matching is still normalized string matching rather than human grading.
