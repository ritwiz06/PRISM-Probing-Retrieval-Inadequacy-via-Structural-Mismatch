- computed RAS margin between the best and second-best backend,

## Known Limitations

- The classifier router is intentionally small and trained only on PRISM's local curated benchmark.
- The keyword router is a transparent baseline, not a production-grade router.
- Computed RAS is perfect on the current combined benchmark, so confidence analysis has no failures to correlate with low-confidence cases.
- The classifier can route very well but is not used in production; computed RAS remains the production router.
- The local SentenceTransformer loader is sensitive to cache environment variables. The router-baseline verifier avoids redirecting `XDG_CACHE_HOME` so Dense stays on `sentence_transformers+faiss`.
