## Known Limitations

- The extracted KG is intentionally simple and rule-based; it is not a broad information-extraction system.
- Extraction only sees facts that are explicitly written in the local corpus.
- Entity and relation normalization use small controlled dictionaries.
- Mixed mode recovers curated performance because the curated KG remains present; this should be interpreted as robustness with added provenance, not as evidence that extraction alone is solved.
- Relational extracted-only results are helped by Hybrid text evidence, so deductive extracted-only results are the clearer structure-shift stress test.
