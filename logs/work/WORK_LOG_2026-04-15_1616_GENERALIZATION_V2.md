## Known Limitations

- The benchmark is public-source-style and normalized locally; it is not a direct full-dataset evaluation from BEIR, NQ, HotpotQA, or WebQSP.
- Items are grounded in PRISM's local corpus/KG so the evaluation remains offline and reproducible.
- The noisy corpus is synthetic; it tests controlled distractor robustness rather than arbitrary web-scale noise.
- Answer accuracy still uses normalized string matching, not human adjudication.
- Computed RAS route accuracy on v2 is below `1.000` because a few held-out lexical-style questions are intentionally phrased less cleanly and route semantically while still answering correctly.
